# Clinics Handoff

## Goal

Design Medical Clinics and Psychology Clinics as operational clinical/admin
workspaces with strong privacy, consent and professional-boundary cues.

## Endpoint Source

Use the `Medical Clinics` and `Psychology Clinics` tags in
`docs/api/openapi.json`.

## Required Screens

1. Clinics product selector.
2. Medical clinic command center.
3. Psychology clinic command center.
4. Patient intake.
5. Appointment/session scheduling.
6. Encounter/session workspace.
7. Clinical note draft packet.
8. Treatment follow-up readiness.
9. Privacy/consent/risk workspace.
10. Records closeout.

## Guardrails

- Clinical notes and assistant outputs must be drafts/review aids.
- Privacy, consent and risk/safety states need prominent visual treatment.
- Growth and billing bridges should be separate from clinical decision surfaces.
